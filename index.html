About WSR

The Weekly Strategic Roundtable (WSR) is an academic think tank platform dedicated to critical analysis of global and regional strategic affairs. It brings together scholars, researchers, and practitioners to engage in meaningful discourse on contemporary security challenges.

Focus Areas

Geopolitics

Strategic shifts, regional conflicts, and global power transitions.

Military Strategy

Modern warfare doctrines, defense systems, and tactical evolution.

Information Warfare

Influence operations, narratives, and cyber strategy.

National Security

Internal and external security policy analysis.
